# Gitkrunixdev — Organizational Profile

**Gitkrunixdev** is a technical organization focused on research, documentation, and cryptographic reconstruction in the context of blockchain and Shamir Secret Sharing schemes.

The organization of the core set and individual research and development repositories that support mathematical validation, proof-of-concept, and reconstruction of sensitive data. The materials available on Gitkrunixdev are for educational, documentary, and experimental purposes only. They do not provide consulting or functional services.

## Scope of Activities

- Shamir Secret Sharing Share Reconstruction
- BIP-39 Mathematical and Test Validation
- Blockchain Data Analysis and Seed Recovery
- Institutional Documentation and Forensic Login
- CLI Tools for Testing and Virus Infection
